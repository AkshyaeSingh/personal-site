Akshyae Singh

Books

Not reviews. Just what stuck.

AI & Alignment

  • Human Compatible
    Stuart Russell
    The clearest argument I've read for why the current paradigm is wrong at its foundations.
  • The Alignment Problem
    Brian Christian
    Good for showing how deep the problem runs — it's not a missing feature, it's everywhere.
  • Superintelligence
    Nick Bostrom
    Dated in places but the core argument still hasn't been answered satisfactorily.

Science & Thinking

  • The Structure of Scientific Revolutions
    Thomas Kuhn
    Changed how I think about what "progress" in a field actually means.
  • Thinking, Fast and Slow
    Daniel Kahneman
    Useful less as a how-to and more as a catalog of ways reasoning goes wrong.

Everything Else

  • The Third Door
    Alex Banayan
    The frame I've actually used. When the front door is blocked, there's always a third door. I tested it outside Paramount HQ at 7 AM.
  • The Master and His Emissary
    Iain McGilchrist
    Dense and occasionally overstated, but genuinely shifted something in how I see attention and culture.
  • Meditations
    Marcus Aurelius
    Not inspiring in the self-help sense. More like a corrective.
