Interests

What I do when I'm not working

Football

Played professionally for Hindustan FC in India's Hero I-League — youngest player in the starting eleven. Was varsity captain in high school. Still play wherever I can. It's the one thing that empties my head completely.

Meditation

Started in my second year at UCLA. College while constantly pushing yourself can be brutal. Meditation was what kept me grounded — it healed and strengthened me more than anything else I've tried. I take it seriously.

Travel

I have a rule: say yes to random trips, especially if you don't know anyone going. That's where the real memories get made. I've lived in Delhi, LA, and Singapore. Each one rewired something.

Community

I've built communities from scratch more times than I can count — and I genuinely love the craft of it. Making rooms where people who should know each other finally do. Theta Chi at UCLA was my first taste of what real brotherhood feels like outside of family.

Other things

Cooking, chess, podcasts, long conversations about the future. I'm more curious than I am expert in most things, which is probably why I keep ending up in new fields.
